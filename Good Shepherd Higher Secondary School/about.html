Our Profile & History

Good Shepherd Higher Secondary School is managed by Good Shepherd Church Chapahati. Our institution stands as a structural symbol of quality education, deep-rooted ethical values, and holistic human growth.

We are wholeheartedly committed to building equitable learning systems. Our systems support all diverse student populations—including Garos, Hajongs, Bengalis, and others—irrespective of local caste, background, or economic status.
